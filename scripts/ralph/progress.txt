## US-603: Add two-finger rotation gesture for selected layer ✅
- Added angle tracking (lastPinchAngleRef) and rotation gesture state refs
- On pointerdown with 2 fingers: hit-tests both fingers against selected layer
- If both fingers hit the layer, starts a 200ms hold timer before activating rotation
- On pointermove: calculates angle delta via Math.atan2, applies to layer rotation
- Snaps to 0/90/180/270 when within 5 degrees for precision
- Quick pinch-zoom (>15% distance change) cancels rotation hold to prevent accidental rotation
- Pushes undo snapshot on first rotation delta for reversibility
- On empty canvas, two fingers pan/zoom as before (no rotation)
